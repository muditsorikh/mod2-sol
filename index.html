Noble Prize Winner in Chemistry 2019

John B Goodenough

The full name is John Bannister Goodenough. He was born in (Jena, Weimar Germany)25 July 1922. He is a material scientist and solid-state physicist, currently he is on the post of professor of mechanical engineering at the University of Texas Austin. He is credited for his work in the development of the LITHIUM ION BATTERY, GOODENOUGH-KANAMORI RULES, and developments in COMPUTER RAM.

M Stanley Whittingham

The full name is Michael Stanley Whittingham. He was born in (Nottingham, England)1941 He is a professor of chemistry and director both at the Institute for Materials Research and the Materials Science and engineering program at Binghamton University. He is mainly known for the development of LITHIUM ION BATTERY.

AKIRA YOSHINO

He was born in (Osaka, Japan) on 30 January 1948. He is a fellow of ASAHI KASEI CORPORATION and professor at Meijo University in Nagoya. He is credited with the first safe production-viable LITHIUM ION BATTERY which is used in mobile phones and laptops.
